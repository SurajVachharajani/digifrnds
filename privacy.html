Cookies Policy
What Are Cookies, Exactly?

Cookies are little files kept on your computer that contain a small amount of information about the websites you visit. These files contain information on the web page you visited as well as any other data you've provided, such as your name and interests.

What Is the Purpose of Cookies?

Cookies allow us to track how you interact with our content and improve your overall experience on our site. We can remember your language option thanks to cookies, so you don't have to change it every time you visit. We can also target adverts on third-party websites based on your behaviour on our website so that we can re-market our products/services to you.

There are also the many types of cookies that you should be aware of.

    Cookies for the duration of a session
  • Session cookies are used to remember who you are when you visit our site. When the web browser is closed, they expire.
    Cookies from the first and third parties
  • On our website, we utilise both first-party and third-party cookies.
    First-party cookies are those that are set by the website you're browsing right now. Third-party cookies are those that come from other websites and are primarily used for advertising purposes.
    Cookies that last a long time
  • Even if you close your web browser or restart your computer, persistent cookies stay on your computer/mobile device. We create a visit pattern based on this information in order to optimise the site's functionality. These cookies are also used for advertising that is more specific to you.
    How Do I Delete And Reject Cookies?
  • Keep in mind that if you disable cookies, you may not be able to get the most out of a website and your experience may be harmed. Simply updating your browser settings will allow you to refuse and erase cookies.
    Visit http://www.allaboutcookies.org/ for more information. to learn how to reject or delete them in further detail.
